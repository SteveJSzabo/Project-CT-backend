Welcome to the official site of the Chosen Travelers
Destiny 2 Clan, home to Guardians from all walks of life. With a focus on both PvP and PvE, we work as one to reach the endgame.

What Chosen Travelers Focus On

PvE

Destiny's sandbox is wide and varied, ranging from large-scale public events to playlist activities. No matter the focus, our goal as a clan is to work together to achieve success, bringing fellow clan members together to have a great time across all seasons and all holiday events.

PvP

Whether casual Quickplay or high-intensity comp, Chosen Travelers is home to skilled PvP players ready and willing to support each other in the Crucible. Working as a six-stack or a three-stack, we push for victory as one!

PvP

Whether casual Quickplay or high-intensity comp, Chosen Travelers is home to skilled PvP players ready and willing to support each other in the Crucible. Working as a six-stack or a three-stack, we push for victory as one!

Endgame

Destiny 2's endgame comprises everything from three-man Grandmaster Nightfalls to six-man raids and, if duels are what we're after, the ultimate PvP conquest of Trials. As a united clan, we work together to reach the pinnacle endgame of Destiny 2!
